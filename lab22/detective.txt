Alpha - Merge sort
	has consistent comparisons accross the board, but with many movements indicating the merging of the smaller lists

Beta - Insertion Sort
	when sorting a sorted listed, there are N comparisons made

Gamma - Quick Sort
	when sorting a sorted list, has n-1 movements with n^2 comparisons

Delta - Heap Sort
	has consistent comparisons accross the board, smaller ratio of movements to comparisons than merge

Epsilon - Selection Sort
	when sorting a sorted list, makes (n * n-1)/2 comparisons with no movement/swaps
